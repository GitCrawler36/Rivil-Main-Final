Scholarships & Funding

Education in New Zealand is an investment in your future. To support high-achieving international students, a range of scholarship programmes are available from the New Zealand Government and from individual universities. Our counsellors will help you identify what you may qualify for — completely free.

Manaaki New Zealand Scholarships

The New Zealand Government’s official scholarship programme for students from eligible countries, covering tuition, living costs and travel for selected programmes of study.

University Merit Scholarships

Most New Zealand universities offer merit-based entrance scholarships for high-achieving international students, including vice-chancellor’s awards for first-year entry.
