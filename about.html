Our Story

21 years of opening doors for Sri Lankan students

Rivil International Education Consultants was established in 2004 with a single, unwavering purpose — to make the world's best universities accessible to Sri Lankan students. What began as a small consultancy in Mount Lavinia has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted names in overseas education, guiding students towards recognised institutions across the United Kingdom, New Zealand, Australia, Canada, Malaysia, Singapore and Dubai.

Driven by our core values of knowledge, experience, and ethics, we have built a reputation on one simple promise — to treat every student as an individual. We understand that each student arrives with a unique combination of qualifications, ambitions, and circumstances, and our experienced counsellors bring patient, personalised attention to every case.

We have never charged a student a single rupee for our services — and we never will. Our guidance is completely free because we believe that access to quality education should not come with a financial barrier. We are funded by our institutional partnerships, not by the students we serve.
