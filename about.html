Who We Are

21 years of opening doors for Sri Lankan students

Rivil International Education Consultants was established in 2004 with a single, unwavering purpose — to make the world's best universities accessible to Sri Lankan students. What began as a small consultancy in Mount Lavinia has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted names in overseas education, guiding students towards recognised institutions across the United Kingdom, New Zealand, Australia, Canada, Malaysia, Singapore, Dubai and Ireland.

Driven by our core values of knowledge, experience, and ethics, we have built a reputation on one simple promise — to treat every student as an individual. We understand that each student arrives with a unique combination of qualifications, ambitions, and circumstances, and our experienced counsellors bring patient, personalised attention to every case.

Director's Message

Welcome to Rivil International Education Consultants.

For over 22 years, we have been driven by one purpose: helping Sri Lankan students pursue world-class education and build successful futures abroad. What began as a vision for trustworthy guidance has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most respected names in international education — built on expertise, integrity, and genuine care for every student we work with.

International education is more than an application or a visa — it is a life-changing journey. That's why every student who walks through our doors receives personalised guidance from a team that stays ahead of an ever-evolving global landscape. Integrity and transparency aren't principles we simply promote; they're the standards we live by.

Our commitment goes beyond admissions — from choosing the right pathway to navigating visas and preparing for life abroad. Our success isn't measured by numbers, but by the futures our students go on to build.

Your future begins with the right guidance. We're honoured to help you take that first step.
