UK study can be supported through government scholarship programmes and awards offered directly by universities. Eligibility, availability and award values vary, and our counsellors can help you identify realistic options for your profile.

The UK Government’s international scholarship programme supports eligible applicants undertaking selected one-year taught master’s degrees.

Students from eligible Commonwealth countries may be able to apply for postgraduate funding, subject to the criteria of each scholarship route.
