Who We Are

Turning Sri Lankan ambitions into global achievements

Rivil International Education Consultants was established in 2004 with a single, unwavering purpose — to make the world's best universities accessible to Sri Lankan students. What began as a small consultancy in Mount Lavinia has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted names in overseas education, guiding thousands of students every year towards recognised institutions across the United Kingdom, New Zealand, Australia, Canada, Malaysia, and Singapore.

Driven by our core values of knowledge, experience, and ethics, we have built a reputation on one simple promise — to treat every student as an individual. We understand that each student arrives with a unique combination of qualifications, ambitions, and circumstances. Our team of experienced education consultants brings patient, personalised attention to every case, ensuring that the guidance you receive is genuinely tailored to you.

We are proud of our outstanding 100% success rate — a record we have maintained consistently year after year. But to us, that figure represents something far more important than a statistic. It represents students who are now living their dreams abroad, building careers, and returning home with internationally recognised qualifications. At Rivil, we do not just process applications — we build bridges between aspirations and achievements.
