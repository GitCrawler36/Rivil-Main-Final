How it works

Seven stages. Zero cost. One clear path abroad.

Here is exactly what happens from the moment you decide to study abroad to the day you fly out — and every step of it is completely free.

  1. 1

    You have a goal — study abroad

    It starts with your ambition. Whether you have a specific country in mind or only a feeling that your future lies overseas, that is enough to begin.

  2. 2

    Explore destinations and universities

    Browse our six destinations — New Zealand, the UK, Australia, Canada, Malaysia and Singapore — and the institutions we work with in each, right here on this site.

    Browse destinations
  3. 3

    Submit an enquiry

    Reach us however suits you — the enquiry form, WhatsApp, a phone call, or a visit to our Mount Lavinia office. Tell us about yourself and where you want to go.

  4. 4

    A counsellor reviews your profile

    One of our counsellors studies your qualifications, goals and budget, and responds within 24 hours with honest, tailored recommendations.

  5. 5

    Guided application preparation

    We help you assemble everything your chosen university needs — documents, statements and forms — checked and polished before anything is sent.

  6. 6

    Application submitted and tracked

    Your application goes in, and we stay on top of it — following up with the institution and keeping you informed at every stage until a decision arrives.

  7. 7

    Offer received — visa and departure

    Congratulations — you are in. We guide you through the visa process and give you a pre-departure briefing so you arrive ready for your first day.

Every step is completely free.

No fees, no commissions, no hidden costs — from your first enquiry to your pre-departure briefing.
